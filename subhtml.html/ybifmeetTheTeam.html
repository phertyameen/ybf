Our Team

Get to know our team of committed experts pushing YBIF foundation ahead with their unmatched knowledge in various fields. Together, they represent the unstoppable force propelling our mission to greater heights.

The chairman/Founder

Yahaya Buhari Ali is a Serial Entrepreneur, Digital marketing expert (Google certified) Brand strategist, Business consultant, Nation builder, philanthropist & CEO YBI GROUP Education Yahaya Buhari obtained his Bsc. Biochemistry from Usmanu Danfodiyo University, Sokoto 2015, he then furthered his education by enrolling in a PG program at kaduna state university and bagged a diploma in entrepreneurship in 2017, he has so many certificates and awards on entrepreneurship and personal development He is the Founder and CEO of YB GROUP 2015 till date. YBI GROUP is one of the biggest startup conglomerate in Nigeria, it comprises of YB INNOVATIONS (Branding, printing and packaging), YBI MULTIMEDIA (Digital media), YB EXPRESS (logistics) and YB CONSULTS (consultancy). It is founded and managed by Yahaya Buhari Ali with a team of about 65 staff, it is located in Kaduna State Nigeria.

The Executive Vice Chairman

Suleiman Abdulmajid is the first Nigerian nominee for the Commonwealth Youth Award of Excellence for his efforts in combatting climate change. A result-oriented community development practitioner, climate change advocate, and a farmer, Suleiman was the first State Coordinator for Nigeria Youth for Peace Initiative, a youth-led organization working on conflict prevention and peacebuilding processes across communities in Nigeria and also works towards localization of the United Nations Security Council Resolution 2250 on Youth peace and security. He is a member of the Nigerian Society of Engineers as well as the Institution of Engineering and Technology. In addition to the Competent Communicator Prize of the Toastmasters International Club, Suleiman is also the winner of the Icon of Hope Award. He also serves as the chief volunteer of the World Merit organization and an ambassador for the International Model of the United Nations. He is a Kashim Ibrahim Fellow and a former Special Assistant to the Governor of Kaduna State. He obtained his PhD Public governance and leadership, UniAbuja 2022-present Master of Leadership Studies, Nigerian Defence Academy of Kaduna State, 2018 Bachelor of Science in Electrical and Electronics Engineering, University of East London Malaysia Campus, 2015 Memberships Member of Nigerian Society of Engineers and Institution of Engineering and Technology, UK. Member, Abuja Leadership Center.
